Teacher Help

The objective of this webquest is to get your students have their first contact with HTML, CSS and Javascript, while building their very own first webpage and also learning a few cool tricks!

The teacher should ideally give the students a few basic concepts of how webpages work, and then let them take on the challenge of the webquest.

Duration

This webquest is intended to be long possibly ranging from 2 to 4 weeks.
